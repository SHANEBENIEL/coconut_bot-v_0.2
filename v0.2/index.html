What actually runs
① AR(7) — Fits OLS regression on lags [pt−1…pt−7] via normal equations X'X·β = X'y solved by Gaussian elimination.
② Holt-Winters — Double exponential smoothing (level + trend). α and β optimised by grid-searching SSE on the training fold.
③ LSTM — Two stacked LSTM layers (16→8 units) + Dense(1), 14-step lookback, trained with Adam (lr=0.005), MSE loss, 15 epochs in TF.js.
Ensemble — Grid-searched weights (step 0.1) minimising validation MAPE. Intervals: empirical ±q80/q95 of |residuals|, scaled by √horizon.
